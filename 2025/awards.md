## About the CVMP Technical Awards

The CVMP Technical Awards celebrate exceptional contributions to the field of visual media production. These awards recognize:

- **Research Impact Award**: Awarded to individual(s) who have performed key research that has been taken by third parties and used effectively in media production or products.

- **Collaboration Award**: Recognizes joint effort on a dedicated project between individuals jointly across both academia and industry.

- **Technology Impact Award**: Awarded to individual(s) who have created a novel tool that has significantly impacted media production.
